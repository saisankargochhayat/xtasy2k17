TERRIBLY TINY TALES

The event consists of three rounds.
Round 1 (Online Round)
5 words will be given and participants have to submit three TTTs based on these five words.
The best 20 TTTs will qualify for Round 2.
Round 2 (TTT Battle)
The 20 contestants selected would be divided into groups of 5.
Each group will have to frame a TTT using the highlighted word from the previous group’s TTT. (Sort of TTT Antakshari).
Each team would be given 2minutes to frame a TTT.
The team with most number of points wins.
Round 3 (Finals)
The five contestants selected from Round 2 would be given 5 words and each participant will have to frame 5TTTs within 30minutes. The participant with best set of TTTs wins.
