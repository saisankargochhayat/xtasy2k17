The Dancing Feat





RULES:

  • The competition invites all dance forms including Hip-hop, Punjabi,Jazz, Kathakali or Contemporary.
  • Participants should get their songs/music in a CD or a pendrive. Live music is not allowed.
  • The maximum time limit of the performance will be minutes 2-3 minutes.
  • Accessories and costumes have to be arranged by the participants.
  • The performance should be in cohesion with the integrity of the fest. The songs selected by the participants must not contain any foul language.
  • Live animals and naked flames are not allowed.
  • Violation of any of the rules mentioned above will lead to immediate disqualification.

    • AFTER EACH COMPETITION GETS OVER , ALL THE PARTICIPANTS WILL BE GIVEN THEIR SCORE CARDS EVALUATED BY THE JUDGES AND THERE WOULD BE AN OPEN DISCUSSION BETWEEN THE PARTICIPANTS AND THE JUDGES .




Burnout - Street Dance Event





PRELIMS:

  • All teams must have at least 4 members. There is no maximum limit.
  • All teams will have a maximum of 8 minutes to complete their routine in the prelims i.e. the showcase round.

FINALS:


  • The teams ranked in the top 4 in the prelims will compete in the finals.

  • Teams qualifying for this round will be going against each other in a face off competition. The order of the team will be decided through a draw system and the battles would be as follows-:

    1. BATTLE 1 – TEAM 1 vs TEAM 2
    2. BATTLE 2 – TEAM 3 vs TEAM 4
    3. FINAL BATTLE – FINAL TEAM X vs FINAL TEAM Y

  • Music track will be provided by the xtasy team on the spot itself.
  • In case of tie, another battle round may conduct.
  • Judging for prelims round will be done over those criteria: costumes, choreography, co-ordination, energy level, creativity and over all impact on the crowd.
  • Judging for battle round will depend upon crowd support & moreover over shadowing the opponent team with your moves.
  • Use of Naked flames and animals are prohibited.
  • Avoiding any of the Rules mentioned above will lead to immediate Disqualification.

    • AFTER EACH COMPETITION GETS OVER , ALL THE PARTICIPANTS WILL BE GIVEN THEIR SCORE CARDS EVALUATED BY THE JUDGES AND THERE WOULD BE AN OPEN DISCUSSION BETWEEN THE PARTICIPANTS AND THE JUDGES .
