Group Dance Event

If synchronization and rhythm are the laws you abide by, this time round, dance not just with your feet but with your heart as well. So, bring along your troop and let the marriage of your moves and rhythm, bewitch your onlookers as never before.

Salsa/ Bollywood Dance Workshop

Salsa: Reawaken the subdued chemistry as we open up a new learning experience for you. Let the drill imparted infuse a spirit of elegant dominance as you learn to go all latino with your moves! ;) Bollywood: If the thumkas and mataks are as appealing to you as the next door gossip, come and join us as the professionals in the art din those bolly moves into you :D

Solo Dance Event

If your feet oblige to tapping to music involuntarily, they are meant to trace graceful motions on the stage. Dance your way through as you let your unique moves do most of the talking and mesmerize the audience with that shake of your leg.

Burnout - Street Dance Event

Why walk across the street when you can dance. Are you ready to sweep people off their feet as you pop and lock yourselves in their hearts? So, claim the street stage, dance your way across and show ‘em who’s the boss.
